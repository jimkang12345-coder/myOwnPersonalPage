About My Education

Middle School and High School

I went to Joennam Middle School for 2 months. Then I enrolled in FCS, Friendship Christian School, in Suwanee, Georgia, for the 7th grade. After, I enrolled in Pincrest Academy in Cumming, Georgia, for the 8th grade.

When I was a sophomore, I left the US and came back to South Korea, then went to Yeongsan Highschool (영산고) and graduated there.

University

I went to HUFS, Hankuk University of Foreign Studies or 한국외국어대학교, for my bachelor's degrees in Spanish and International Studies.

After, I went to SU, Stockholm University, in Sweden to study master's program in Latin American Studies.
